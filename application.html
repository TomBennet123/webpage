Your Application Journey: A Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare & Submit Your Documents

Download the application form and gather the required documents (see the checklist below). Email your complete package to the faculty coordinator at facultyofengineering@mk.uni-pannon.hu.

Step 2: Await Confirmation

The faculty reviews your documents and confirms whether you meet the entry requirements. Do not pay the application fee until you receive approval.

Step 3: Pay Application Fee

After approval, transfer the non-refundable 150 EUR application fee and email the proof of payment to the coordinator. Deadlines: 30th April (Autumn intake) or 15th October (Spring intake).

Step 4: Online Entrance Exam

Participate in the online entrance exam organized by the faculty. You will receive the technical details and schedule via email.

Step 5: Secure Your Place (Successful Applicants)

If you pass the admission process, pay the tuition fees for the first two semesters. If you request dormitory accommodation, also transfer the 300 EUR deposit.

Step 6: Enrolment

Arrive in person during the registration week (the first week of the semester) and present your original documents for verification.

Required Documents Checklist

Prepare the following documents for your application. Double-check that every item is included before you submit your package.

  • Completed Application Form - Request from the faculty coordinator.
  • High School/University Degree - Copy with official English translation.
  • Transcript of Records - Copy of previous studies with official English translation.
  • Proof of English Proficiency - TOEFL (min. 60), IELTS (min. 5.5), or an Attestation if previous studies were in English.
  • Passport/ID Card - Copy of a valid passport, or a valid ID/driving licence for EU citizens.
  • CV in English.
  • Motivation Letter in English - Maximum 3000 characters.
  • Letters of Recommendation (Optional) - Submitting these is an advantage.

Important Rules & Deadlines

Document Corrections

If you are asked to correct a document, you have 8 days to do so. Failure to correct it within the deadline will result in your application being rejected.

Payment Deadline

The application fee must be transferred by 30th April (Autumn Intake) or 15th October (Spring Intake) at the latest.

Visa Process

Successful applicants should initiate visa procedures immediately after receiving the acceptance letter to ensure timely arrival in Hungary.

General Information

Official Communication

Official communication takes place via email. Make sure you regularly check your inbox (and spam folder) for updates.

Academic Year

The academic year has two semesters. The Fall semester begins in early September, and the Spring semester begins in early February. Each semester is 14 weeks long, followed by a 6-week examination period.

Entrance Requirements

Some programmes may require additional documents or interviews. Always follow the specific instructions provided by the faculty.

Accommodation

Full-time students in Veszprém can apply for accommodation in university dormitories. For more information, visit the official website.

Visa & Refunds

If your study visa application is rejected, pre-paid tuition and accommodation fees will be refunded if you cancel before the term begins.

Postponing Studies

Admitted students may request to postpone the start of their studies for up to one year, subject to faculty approval.

Living in Veszprém

Veszprém offers a vibrant student life with easy access to Lake Balaton. Our international office provides guidance on settling in and everyday practicalities.
