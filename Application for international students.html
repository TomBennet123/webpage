Application for international students

Application for The Self-Funded Programmes

Application Periods:

1st February – 30th April (Autumn semester)

15th August – 15th October (Spring semester)

The self-funded application process includes the following steps:

  • Submit the requested documents to the chosen faculty
  • Transfer the non-refundable application fee (150 EUR) deadline: 30th April or 15th October
  • Participate in the online entrance exam organised by the Faculty
  • Successful applicants are required to transfer the tuition fee for the first 2 semesters to secure their place (deadline: 15 days after issuing the Notification Letter but not later than 10th July or 1st December)
  • If you require dormitory accommodation, 300 EUR must be pre-paid at the same time as the tuition fee covering the first 2 months of the semester.
  • Finish your enrollment during the registration week (the first week of the semester or 30th September or 1st March at the latest)

Basic application documents:

1. A completed application form can be requested from faculty coordinators in case of self-funded application at facultyofengineering@mk.uni-pannon.hu

2. Copy of high school or university degree (with an official translation into English)

3. Copy of the Transcript of Records of previous studies (including the list of complete courses/subjects and the results) (with an official translation into English)

4. Certificate of English language knowledge:

  • Copy of TOEFL iBT certificate with a minimum score of 60; OR
  • Copy of IELTS certificate with a minimum score of 5.5; OR
  • If the applicant completed his/her previous studies in an institution where the language of instruction was English, the applicant may present an original Attestation of Language Proficiency.

5. Copy of the valid passport (with the place and date of expiration) OR in case of EU citizenship, copy of a valid ID card or driving licence.

6. CV in English

7. Motivation letter in English (max. 3000 characters)

8. Submitting letters of recommendation is an advantage

Also, you can find more information about the programmes here: https://mk.uni-pannon.hu/index.php/en/academic-programmes

The prerequisites and credit requirements for the programmes can be found under each programme’s description on the website.

Once, with a deadline of 8 days, the applicant may be requested to correct any deficiencies due to a technical problem. After the 8-day deadline, if the incorrect documents have not been corrected, the application will be rejected by the faculty on the grounds of a formal error.

Of the documents that are part of the admission procedure, only the documents in proof of qualification may be substituted. After the deadline for submitting missing documents (15th July or 10th December), the faculty will reject the applicant and close the admission procedure if the missing documents are not submitted.

If no payment is made after the deadline for tuition fee payment, the faculty will reject the applicant and close the application (deadline: 15 days after issuing the Notification Letter but not later than 10th July or 1st December).

In case of successful admission, you must present your original documents during the enrollment process.

The academic year:

Each academic year consists of two 14-week long semesters followed by a 6-week examination period each. The fall semester starts at the beginning of September. Spring semester usually starts at the beginning of February.

Accommodation:

Additional information:

If a student does not receive a study visa from their country, the pre-paid tuition and accommodation fees will be refunded if the student cancels their reservation before the start of the term.

You can cancel the dormitory application via e-mail until 26th August 2024. After that date, the university cannot refund the student’s dormitory deposit.

Admitted students may, upon request and with the consent of the faculty, postpone the start of their studies for up to 1 year.
